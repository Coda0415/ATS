About Frantz Building Services

Who we are:

Frantz Building Services, a commercial cleaning company in the heart of the Midwest, is currently hiring key positions. Frantz offers entry-level cleaner to managerial positions in Kentucky, Indiana, Ohio, and Tennessee.

  • We value competitive pay and benefits.
  • We value a promotion from within philosophy.
  • We value a small company feel and team mindset.
  • We value enriching the lives of our Team Members both personally and professionally.

If you are positive, upbeat, have an eye for detail, and a desire to serve customers, we would love to have you on our team. If you want a consistent, stable, and secure position with a best-in-class company that provides meaningful work while building meaningful relationships, Frantz Building Services should be your new work home!  We value qualities like integrity, humility, excellence, and allowing our team to work with an ownership mentality (no micromanaging here!).

Job Details

What the job looks like:

  • Cleans floors, surfaces equipment, linens, towels, and beds.
  • Routinely cleans and sanitizes patient rooms, nursing units, administrative offices, and waiting areas.
  • Daily inventories of cleaning supplies and equipment as well as inspecting equipment for any repairs or replacements.
  • Adheres to all infection control and isolation procedures to ensure a safe work environment.
  • Understands and demonstrates proper usage of all cleaning chemicals in accordance with the MSDS product standards and Community Hospice procedures. Don’t worry; we’ll teach you!
  • Completes all scheduled daily assignments within designated time frames. Informs manager at the end of the day of any assignments not completed.
  • Other responsibilities as assigned.
